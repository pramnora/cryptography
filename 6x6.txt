There 26 alphabet letters: A-Z/and, 10 digits: 0-9; 
this makes for a total of 36 characters, altogether: (26+10=36); 
each character can be placed into a 6 x 6 grid: 6 x 6 = 36.

    a   b   c   d   e   f

a   A   B   C   D   E   F
 
b   G   H   I   J   K   L

c   M   N   O   P   Q   R

d   S   T   U   V   W   X

e   Y   Z   0   1   2   3

f   4   5   6   7   8   9

So, to encode the text: Paul; 
we first find: P...;
then, read off the row/col letters: c,d;
next, find: A...;
then, read off the row/col letters: a,a;
next, find: U...;
then, read off the row/col letters: d,c;
next, find: L...;
then, read off the row/col letters: b,f;
thus, Paul encoded becomes:
cdaadcbf
